Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 13, 2026

Overview

ReRoot ("the App") is a tobacco cessation support application for iOS. Your privacy is fundamental to our design. This policy explains what data the App accesses, how it is handled, and your rights.

Data We Collect

ReRoot does not collect, transmit, or store any personal data on external servers. All user data remains entirely on your device.

  • Check-in data (mood, lapse status, craving level, triggers) — stored on-device via encrypted local storage.
  • Journal entries and voice memos — stored on-device only. Voice memos are transcribed using Apple's on-device Speech framework and are never uploaded.
  • Health data (heart rate variability, sleep, steps, active minutes) — read from Apple HealthKit with your explicit permission. This data is never transmitted off your device.
  • Location data — used in real-time to find nearby support resources via Apple MapKit. Your location is never stored, logged, or transmitted to any server.

AI Chat Feature

The App includes an optional AI support chat powered by Anthropic's Claude API. When you use this feature:

  • Your messages are sent to Anthropic's API to generate responses.
  • No conversation history is saved after you leave the chat screen.
  • Anthropic's data handling is governed by their privacy policy.
  • You must provide your own API key to use this feature. Your API key is stored securely in the iOS Keychain and is never transmitted to anyone other than Anthropic.

Third-Party Services

  • Anthropic (Claude API) — processes AI chat messages only when you actively use the chat feature. No data is sent at any other time.
  • Apple HealthKit — health data is read locally with your permission. Apple prohibits apps from sharing HealthKit data with third parties.
  • Apple MapKit — location searches are processed by Apple's services. See Apple's privacy policy.

ReRoot uses no analytics, no tracking, no advertising SDKs, and no crash reporting services.

Data Storage & Security

  • All data is stored on-device using UserDefaults with JSON encoding and iOS file-level encryption (NSFileProtectionComplete).
  • API keys are stored in the iOS Keychain with kSecAttrAccessibleWhenUnlockedThisDeviceOnly protection.
  • Voice memo audio files are stored in the app's temporary directory and cleaned up after use.

Children's Privacy

ReRoot is not intended for children under 17. We do not knowingly collect any data from children.

Your Rights

Since all data is stored locally on your device, you have full control. You can delete all app data at any time by deleting the App from your device. No data exists on any external server to request deletion of.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected on this page with an updated effective date.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please contact us at support@devcloudesia.com.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I set up the AI chat?

The AI chat feature requires an Anthropic API key. You can get one for free at console.anthropic.com. Enter your key in the App's chat settings — it's stored securely in your device's Keychain.

Is my data private?

Yes. All check-in data, journal entries, and health data stay entirely on your device. Nothing is sent to external servers except AI chat messages (only when you actively use the chat). See our Privacy Policy for full details.

Does ReRoot work without an internet connection?

Yes. All features work offline except the AI chat (which requires an internet connection to reach Anthropic's API) and the nearby support map (which requires location services).

How do I delete my data?

Since all data is stored locally, deleting the App from your device removes all data permanently. There is no cloud backup or account to delete.

I'm in crisis — what should I do?

Please reach out to one of these free, confidential services immediately:

  • 988 Suicide & Crisis Lifeline: Call or text 988 — available 24/7
  • 1-800-QUIT-NOW: Free quit coaching and cessation support
  • Crisis Text Line: Text HOME to 741741
