| 1-8 hours | Carbon Monoxide Is Leaving Your Blood | CO levels in your blood are dropping by half right now. Oxygen is reaching your tissues more efficiently than it has in years. ## 4. Five-Minute Boost — Rotating Messages

These cycle every ~50 seconds during the 5-minute craving timer:

1. Every second you hold is a neural vote against the addiction loop.
2. This craving started 2 minutes ago. It's already getting weaker.
3. The chemical demanding nicotine is an echo of an old habit. Echoes fade.
4. Your body is doing something remarkable right now: learning to function without a drug.
5. The smoker you were would have given up by now. You are not that person anymore.
6. Cravings peak at 3 minutes. You are already past the hardest moment.

## 11. Craving Missions (SOS popup — shuffled)

1. Walk outside for 5 minutes — change your environment
2. Drink a large glass of ice water slowly
3. Eat something crunchy: carrot, apple, celery
4. Call or text someone you trust right now
5. Put on a song you love and listen to the whole thing
6. Do 20 jumping jacks or 10 push-ups
7. Write down 3 reasons you quit
8. Do the 4-7-8 breathing technique twice
9. Brush your teeth or use strong mouthwash
10. Count backwards from 100 by 7s
